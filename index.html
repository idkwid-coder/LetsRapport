Why LetsRapport Matters for Social Good

Reduces Anxiety

For foster youth, much of the stress comes from the fear of a new environment. This app suppresses that anxiety by creating a sense of welcoming pre-arrival.

Promotes Connection

Youth can get a feel for the foster family's home, rules, values, and routines through media profiles before meeting, fostering familiarity.

Empowers Foster Youth

The platform puts youth in a more active role in the process by providing information and giving them some control over the transition experience.
